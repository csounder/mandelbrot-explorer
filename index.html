Dr.C - Mandelbrot Explorer

Where Fractals Become Music

Transform Mathematical Beauty into Sound

A real-time fractal visualization and sonification engine powered by Csound WASM. Explore the infinite depths of the Mandelbrot and Julia sets while mapping fractal geometry to musical parameters through sophisticated synthesis algorithms.

Features

🎨

Real-Time Fractal Visualization

Explore Mandelbrot and Julia sets with smooth zooming, panning, and animation. Choose from 6 stunning color schemes including Fire, Rainbow, Ocean, and Psychedelic.

🎹

11 Synthesis Algorithms

Pluck, FM Bell, Additive Bell, Waveshaping, Organ, Granular, Filtered Noise, Subtractive (Moog), FM Mallet, Waveguide String, and Supersaw synthesis.

🎼

Musical Quantization

Map fractal data to scales (Major, Minor, Pentatonic, Blues, Modes, Microtonal) and rhythmic grids (Whole notes to 128th notes, dotted, and triplets).

✨

Global Effects

Professional-grade Reverb, Echo, Chorus, and Flanger with real-time parameter control and fractal-to-effect mapping.

🎚️

Parameter Mapping

Map 6 fractal parameters (Iteration, Position X/Y, Smoothness, Angle, Magnitude) to 8 synthesis targets and 6 effect parameters.

💾

45 Factory Presets

3 presets per instrument and 2 per visual style, professionally designed to showcase the sonic possibilities. Save unlimited custom presets.

⚡

Csound WASM v7

Real-time audio synthesis in the browser with zero latency using the latest Csound WebAssembly technology.

🎲

Randomize & Explore

One-click randomization of all parameters for instant sonic discovery. Perfect for finding unexpected musical combinations.

Technical Specifications

Audio Engine Csound WASM 7.0.0-beta26 with functional syntax
Sample Rate 44.1 kHz, 32 samples per k-cycle
Synthesis Types 11 unique synthesis algorithms
Effects 4 global effects buses (Reverb, Echo, Chorus, Flanger)
Scales 20 scale types including 12-TET and microtonal systems
Rhythms 25 rhythmic quantization options
Fractal Types Mandelbrot Set and Julia Set with infinite zoom
Rendering CPU Canvas 2D and GPU WebGL modes
