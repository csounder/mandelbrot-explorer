# Quick Start - Dr.C Mandelbrot Explorer v2.0

Get started in 60 seconds!

## 🚀 Launch

Open **`app.html`** in your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)

## 🎯 First Steps

### 1. Initialize Audio (5 seconds)

- Click **"🔊 Initialize Csound"**
- Wait for green status: "Csound WASM ready!"

### 2. Explore Fractals (30 seconds)

- **Click anywhere** on the Mandelbrot set to zoom in
- Watch it recalculate in real-time
- Try the **Julia Set** mode and click to morph

### 3. Add Sound (10 seconds)

- Click **"▶ Play Audio"**
- Hear the fractal as music!
- Adjust **Master Volume** and **Reverb Mix**

### 4. Animate (15 seconds)

- Click **"▶ Start Animation"** (Julia Animation section)
- Watch the Julia set morph automatically
- Try different patterns: Circular, Lissajous, Spiral

## 🎨 Try These Presets

Click these buttons to see beautiful fractals instantly:

**2D Presets** (bottom of left panel):

- **Julia 1** - Classic dragon curve
- **Spiral** - Mesmerizing rotation
- **Dendrite** - Tree-like branching

**3D Mode** (click "3D" tab at top):

- **Bulb Power 8** - Classic Mandelbulb
- **Mandelbox** - Intricate recursive structure

## 🎵 Generate Music

1. Explore until you find an interesting fractal view
2. Scroll to bottom of left panel
3. Click **"Generate .CSD File"**
4. Copy the output (full Csound composition!)
5. Save as `fractal.csd` and render with Csound

## 🎬 Record a Journey

1. Click **"⏺ Start Recording"**
2. Zoom and explore for 30 seconds
3. Click **"⏹ Stop Recording"**
4. Name your path (e.g., "My First Journey")
5. Click **▶** to play it back!

## 💾 Save Your Work

- **Save Image**: Click "💾 Save Image" button
- **Export Csound**: Click "Generate .CSD File" and copy text
- **Paths**: Saved in browser (recorded paths persist in session)

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

**No sound?**

- Click "Initialize Csound" first
- Check browser audio permissions
- Safari: interact with page before initializing

**Slow rendering?**

- Lower "Max Iterations" (try 50-100)
- Switch from GPU to CPU mode (or vice versa)
- Close other browser tabs

**Animation choppy?**

- Reduce "Speed" slider
- Lower "Max Iterations"
- Try CPU mode instead of GPU

## 🌟 Pro Tips

1. **GPU mode** = faster for deep zoom
2. **High iterations** (300+) = beautiful detail
3. **Animation + Audio** = mesmerizing experience
4. **Record interesting zoom sequences** for reproducible results
5. **Export .csd files** for high-quality offline rendering
