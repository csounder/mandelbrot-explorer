Getting Started

1 Launch the Application

Open app-v2.1.html in a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge). The application loads entirely in your browser - no installation required.

Wait for initialization: You'll see "Csound initializing..." in the Audio Control section. After a few seconds, it will change to "Csound WASM ready!"

2 Start Audio

Click the ▶ Play Audio button at the top of the left column. The button will change to ⏸ Stop Audio and you'll hear the fractal being sonified in real-time.

💡 Tip: You can toggle audio on/off at any time by pressing Spacebar or clicking the button.
3 Explore Factory Presets

The easiest way to experience the full range of sounds is to load factory presets:

  1. Find the Presets section in the left column
  2. Click the Saved Presets dropdown - you'll see 45 factory presets
  3. Select a preset (try "FM Bell: Shimmer" or "Pluck: Ambient Sparkle")
  4. Click 📂 Load

The fractal visualization and all audio parameters will instantly update to match the preset.

4 Interact with the Fractal

The fractal responds to mouse/trackpad interaction:

  • Click on the fractal to zoom in (Mandelbrot) or morph the Julia parameter
  • Mousewheel/Trackpad scroll to zoom in/out smoothly
  • Zoom buttons in the left panel for precise control
  • Reset View button returns to the starting position
💡 Listen: As you zoom and move around the fractal, the audio changes in real-time because different fractal regions trigger different notes and parameters!
5 Experiment with Settings

Try changing these key parameters to shape your sound:

Synthesis Type

Choose from 11 algorithms: Pluck, FM Bell, Additive, Organ, Granular, and more

Key & Scale

Set a musical key (C, D, E, etc.) and scale (Major, Minor, Pentatonic, Blues)

Rhythm

Quantize durations to musical note values (Quarters, Eighths, Triplets)

Effects

Add Reverb, Echo, Chorus, or Flanger to create space and depth

6 Randomize for Discovery

Want instant sonic exploration? Click the 🎲 Randomize All button in the left column.

This randomizes:

  • Synthesis algorithm
  • Musical key and scale
  • Rhythmic quantization
  • Tempo and pitch range
  • All effects settings
  • Parameter mappings

Keep clicking until you find something you love, then save it as a preset!

Tips for Best Results

💡 Start with Presets: The 45 factory presets are professionally designed to sound great. Use them as starting points for your own explorations.
💡 Experiment with Scales: Try different combinations of Key + Scale. C# Pentatonic Major, E Phrygian, and A Minor all have unique flavors.
💡 Layer Effects: Reverb creates space, Echo adds rhythmic interest, Chorus thickens the sound, and Flanger adds movement.
💡 Zoom Slowly: The most interesting sounds often emerge when you zoom gradually into the fractal, revealing new boundary regions.
⚠️ Volume Warning: Some randomized settings or presets can be loud. Start with Master Volume at 50% and adjust as needed.

Recording Your Performance

Capture your live fractal audio session as a complete Csound .csd file that can be played in CsoundQt, Csound CLI, or Cabbage:

1

Start Recording

Click "🔴 Start CSD Recording". The complete orchestra (all 11 instruments and 4 effects) is written immediately to the output window.

2

Perform

Click "▶ Play Audio" and interact with the fractal. Every note you hear is captured with accurate timing and all parameter values.

3

Finalize

Click "⏹ Stop CSD Recording". Closing tags are added and a dialog asks if you want to download the file.

4

Save

Click "OK" to download, or "💾 Download .CSD File" later. File saves as synthtype_YYYY-MM-DD_HH-MM-SS.csd

💡 Perfect Playback: The exported .csd file includes all control channel settings, effect parameters, and real-time timestamps. It will sound identical to your browser performance when played in Csound!

Troubleshooting

No Sound?

  • Check that audio is playing (button shows "⏸ Stop Audio")
  • Verify Master Volume is above 0%
  • Make sure your system volume is up
  • Try refreshing the page and restarting audio

Csound Won't Initialize?

  • Make sure you're using a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Check browser console (F12) for error messages
  • Try a hard refresh: Ctrl+Shift+R (Cmd+Shift+R on Mac)

Presets Not Loading?

  • Refresh the page - factory presets load automatically
  • Check browser console for localStorage errors
  • Try clearing browser cache and reloading (factory presets will regenerate)
